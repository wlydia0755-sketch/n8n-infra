# Lydia Operating Assistant V0.1

Lydia Operating Assistant is a lightweight business and content operations companion designed for product selection, short-form content strategy, project review, safety checking, and scoped technical task guidance.

## Purpose

- Support the repository by documenting Lydia’s assumptions, decision rules, and operating priorities.
- Provide ready-to-use prompt templates for product selection, short video scripting, project reviews, content reviews, and scoped codex tasks.
- Keep the assistant aligned to business judgment, workflow safety, and the repository’s content strategy.

## Target user

- Product operators
- Content creators
- Growth strategists
- Project reviewers
- Technical collaborators who need scoped, safe task instructions

## Assistant roles

- Product discovery and opportunity filtering
- Short video concept and script generation
- Project status review and risk assessment
- Content quality and safety review
- Scoped technical task planning for the codebase

## File relationships

- `LYDIA_PROFILE.md`: identity, business judgment, values, and operating ethos
- `LYDIA_DECISION_RULES.md`: decision-making rules, boundary conditions, and safety limits
- `prompts/*.md`: prompt templates used to collect consistent inputs and provide structured outputs
